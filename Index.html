About Me

I’m Franco Pacheco, an Audio Engineer and Music Producer with over 10 years of experience in the industry. My journey started with a deep passion for sound, and over the years, I've had the privilege of working on a wide range of projects—from music albums and EPs to podcasts and audiobooks.

Whether it’s mixing a track to perfection, designing soundscapes for a podcast, or mastering an album for release, I’m dedicated to delivering the highest quality audio that enhances the creative vision of every project. I’ve worked with incredible artists and producers, helping them bring their sound to life with clarity, warmth, and depth.

With a keen ear for detail, I’m always looking for ways to push the boundaries of audio engineering, creating immersive sound experiences that resonate with listeners. From the first recording to the final mastering, I bring both technical expertise and artistic vision to every project.
